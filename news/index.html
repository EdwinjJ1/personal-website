Shortens long ODE trajectories into 2 steps via consecutive leaps, enabling direct reward gradient propagation to early steps. Outperforms GRPO and direct-gradient methods on Flux fine-tuning. CVPR 2026.

→ Paper
SignThought — Latent Thought Reasoning for Gloss-Free Sign Language Translation
Reframes SLT as cross-modal reasoning with ordered latent thoughts as middle layer. Plan-then-ground decoding improves coherence and faithfulness. New large-scale gloss-free dataset. ACL 2026.
→ Paper
Heartbeat-Driven Autonomous Thinking for LLM Agents
Periodic heartbeat mechanism orchestrates Planner, Critic, Recaller, Dreamer modules proactively. Meta-learning for continual policy adaptation. Moves agents from reactive to proactive self-regulation.
→ Paper
LLMs Gaming Verifiers: RLVR Leads to Reward Hacking
RLVR-trained models (GPT-5, Olmo3) systematically abandon genuine rule induction, instead enumerating instance-level labels to game verifiers. This shortcut is absent in non-RLVR models and worsens with task complexity. Isomorphic Perturbation Testing (IPT) detects exploits.
→ Paper
Context Over Content: Exposing Evaluation Faking in LLM Judges
LLM judges exhibit leniency bias when told low scores cause model retraining — peak -9.8pp drop in unsafe-content detection. The judge's chain-of-thought contains zero acknowledgment of this bias, making it invisible to standard CoT inspection.
→ Paper
When Flat Minima Fail: INT4 Quantization Collapse After FP32 Convergence
Auditing all 154 Pythia-160m checkpoints reveals three-phase divergence: after FP32 converges, INT4 gap explodes from 11% to 517%. INT8 is immune. Proposed Oscillatory Lock-In schedule reduces INT4 gap by 2.2pp.
→ Paper
SpecGuard: Verification-Aware Speculative Decoding for Reasoning
Step-level verification using attention grounding + log-probability confidence. Achieves +3.6% accuracy and -11% latency over standard speculative decoding on reasoning benchmarks. No external reward model needed.
